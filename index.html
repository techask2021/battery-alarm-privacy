Privacy Policy for Battery Alarm

Last updated: [INSERT DATE]

This Privacy Policy describes how Battery Alarm ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Battery Alarm collects the following information from your device:

  • Battery Information: Battery level (percentage), charging state, battery health metrics (temperature, voltage, capacity)
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Charging session history, battery usage patterns (stored locally on your device)
  • App Settings: Your notification preferences, target charge levels, monitoring settings

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information directly from your device using Android/iOS system APIs. We do not collect any personal information that you manually enter.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Monitor battery charging status in real-time
  • Send notifications when your target charge level is reached
  • Track charging history for battery health optimization
  • Provide battery health insights and recommendations
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All battery data, charging history, and app settings are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your battery data to external servers.

Data Security: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information about your device and app usage to provide personalized ads.

Information collected by AdMob may include:

  • Advertising ID (resettable)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Location data (if permissions granted)
  • App usage data

For more information about how Google AdMob collects and uses data, please visit: Google AdMob Privacy Policy

You can opt-out of personalized ads by adjusting your device's advertising settings.

Google Play Services

Our app uses Google Play Services for app functionality. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information about their data practices.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Google AdMob (as described above) for advertising purposes
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

7. Data Retention

Charging session history is stored locally on your device and can be deleted at any time through the app settings. We keep the last 100 charging sessions. You can clear all data at any time using the "Clear All Data" option in settings.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View your charging history and app data within the app
  • Deletion: Delete your data using the "Clear History" or "Clear All Data" options in settings
  • Opt-out: Opt-out of personalized ads via your device's advertising settings
  • Data Portability: Export your charging history data (feature available in app)

9. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
